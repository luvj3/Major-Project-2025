Welcome to WhatsApp Chat Analyzer!

This tool helps you analyze your WhatsApp conversations with detailed statistics and visualizations.

How to use:

  1. Export your WhatsApp chat (without media) from your WhatsApp mobile app
  2. Upload the exported text file using the sidebar
  3. Click "Process Chat Data" to analyze your conversation
  4. Explore the various metrics and visualizations

How to export your WhatsApp chat:

  1. Open the chat you want to analyze
  2. Tap the three dots (menu) in the top right
  3. Select "More" > "Export chat"
  4. Choose "Without media"
  5. Save or share the file to your device
